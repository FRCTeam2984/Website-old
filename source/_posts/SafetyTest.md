Safety Test Instructions
===
Read through the [Study Guide](MTT_STUDY_GUIDE.pdf) before you take the exam.

Question number 3 has the choices listed wrong. There are two answers labeled b) . Change the second b) to c) and the c) to d).

There are a sires of problem which you have to skip in this exam. You can skip these problems:
60-69. Problem 70 will be 60 on your answer sheet.
84-88. Problem 89 will be 74 on your answer sheet.
90-93. Problem 94 will be 75 on your answer sheet.
96-112. Problem 113 will be 77 on your answer sheet.
This makes for a total of 78 problems on the exam.
Good Luck!
